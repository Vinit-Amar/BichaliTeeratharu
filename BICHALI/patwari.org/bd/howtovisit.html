How to Visit BICHALI – BHIKSHAALAYA

 

Direction from Mantralaya to Bichali (Sacred Bhikshaalaya Bichali is about twenty kilometers from Mantralaya)

·        From Mantralaya we have to come to Madhavaram and turn to the right. The right road leads to Raichur

·        After crossing the Tungabhadra river bridge we get Gillesugur camp.

·        Turn to the left at Gillesugur camp (Gillesugur camp is 13 kilometers from Mantralaya)

·        From Gillesugur camp Bichali is only seven kilometeres.

·        The straight road takes us to Bichali Japadakatti. Now the road is very fine.
