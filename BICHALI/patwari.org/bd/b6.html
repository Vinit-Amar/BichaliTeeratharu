Bhikshaalaya Darshana-6

(Diwan Venkanna's preparations for the stay of Rayary in Mantralaya)

Date : 27 July 2006

Background

Diwan Venkanna made elaborate preparations for the stay of our Sri Raghavendrateertharu in Mantralaya. Frequent visit of Rayaru Bichchale for Sri Appanacharya,Japadakatte and the miracle of Rayaru are described in Bhikshaalaya(6).
 

Meaning

Diwan Venkanna took keen interest in making arrangements for the stay of Sri Raghavendra Gurusarvabhoumaru in Mantralaya. He invited sadhus,sajjanas and the brahmins. Built the Village Manchale(Mantralaya) on a much elevated place, that the overflow and spate of Tungabhadra river should not harm,as there was no Tungabhadra dam during those days.Sri Rayaru felt extremely happy in Mantralaya and desired to reside in Yogamudra - in Brindavana in Mantralaya. Venkanna constructed  the temple of Sri Tirumala Srinivasa Devaru - the Kula Swami of our Rayaru and Rayaru made the Pratishthhaapana of Srinivasa Devaru in Mantralaya. Kula Swami Sri Venkateshwara and Kula Devi Manchalemma  temples are the main attraction in Mantralaya to the devotees of Mantralaya Rayaru.

Sri Rayaru lived in Mantralaya with satisfaction.Venkanna was visiting twice in a week and looking after the Sri Matha and Rayaru. Whenever Rayaru thought of  Appanacharya, Rayaru was going to Bichchale frequently irrespective of time and day. The Heart of Rayaru was always throbbing for Bichchale Appanacharya.Similar was the state of heart and mind of Appanacharya. Appanacharya insisted, requested Rayaru to sit on the SACRED JAPADAKATTE in Bichchale and he used to squat on the feet of Rayaru with devotion and obedience, surrendered completely. Their  talk,  interaction were so affectionate ! Today  we feel  that how lucky is our Appanacharya lived and stayed with Rayaru. Appanacharya has completely  and  fully understood Rayaru as mentioned in Gurustotra: APAEKSHITA PRADAATAANYO RAGHAVENDRAAN NA VIDYATAE -  whatever we desire, ask, wish Rayaru will fulfill.In this regard Rayaru is second to none. This is really true.

Within a fortnight we are celebrating the Aaradhana of 335th.year’s. Even to this day the sentence of  Sri Appanacharya’s Gurustotra holds valid, that too in the United States of America.  A sober couple Sri Moka Nagarajarao and Kum.Sow. Raghamma of Raichur, very near to Mantralaya lived in Santee,CA,on the bank of Pacific Ocean. Great devotees of Rayaru residing there always remember Rayaru daily pronouncing Mantralaya not less than 20 times. Sri Moka Nagarajarao performs Rayara Ashtottara on every Thursday. Owing to the oldage,poor health problem of Moka Nagarajarao Sow.Raghamma wished for Muttaidae Bhaagya - she should expire before her husband and should never get   widowship (vaidhavya). With this intention,desire,apaeksha Raghamma prayed Mantralaya Rayaru for Mukti-end of her life, as all the daughters are settled and grand children are all fine.

Though the distance is about of 30,000 miles Rayaru listened the prayer of Raghamma. Her devotional routine,character and always doing good to the others were all noticed by Rayaru. Though she was healthy, suddenly she became sick and felt the shortage of oxygen while breathing .Due to the advanced facilities in the field of medical science lived, carried oxygen for a few months. Prayed Rayaru to take her to his feet that too on Thursday. On that Thursday July 27th  Raghamma listened Sri Appanacharya’s Stotra Ashtottara by her husband. As the day was going she went on becoming weak, pale looked at Rayara Photo, smiled, saw all her people and asked to pour Ganga Jala in her mouth by her relatives and said I am going towards the feet of  Rayaru. Very happily,calmly Raghamma breathed her last breath, On Thursday Shraavana Shukla Dwiteeya in the Magha Nakshatra, when  Mantralaya Rayara Aaradhana is only a fortnight. She obtained Moksha-Sadgati-Muttaidae Bhaagya by Mantralaya Rayaru as per her  Desire:APAEKSHITAPRADAATAANYO RAGHAVENDRAAN NA VIDAYATAE
